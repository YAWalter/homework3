Why is Yonah at NJIT?

Well, for starters, Yonah bleeds New Jersey. Originally from Highland Park, NJ, when choosing schools for undergrad he was faced with the choice between Rutgers and the University of Maryland. Heart-wrenchingly choosing to join the Terrapins, Yonah now finds himself among Highlanders once again.

...but who is Yonah, anyway?

After completing a Bachelors' in Communication (the people kind, not the computer kind), Yonah immediately began working as a technical consultant, specializing in technical communication. Several development jobs later, including crash courses in multiple coding languages, Yonah now finds himself wanting the IT bona fides he has lacked until now.

Yonah has developed user interfaces and functional code for numerous database applications, including a NASA real-property inventory, a contract management tool, a document approvals-tracking system for the Navy, and a blood-glucose meter.

  • English
  • Hebrew
  • Programming:
    • C
    • PHP/MySQL
    • Python
    • Java/JavaScript

That's nice, but why NJIT?

A technical certificate, from a highly respected technical program (like NJIT's), solidifies my own knowledge of the subject matter and prepares me for the next-level challenges. The knowledge I pick up in this program will probably be used to become a Technical Project Lead or CIO.
